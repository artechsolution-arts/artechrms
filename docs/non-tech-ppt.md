- Information lives everywhere — spreadsheets, emails, files, chats

## Slide 4 — The Standout Features

- 🧭 **Guided onboarding ("Start Journey")** — new hires **download → sign → upload** every document, tracked with a progress bar. No more chasing paperwork.
- ✅ **Smart approvals** — requests always reach the right person: employee → HR/CEO; HR's own request → CEO only. Nobody approves their own.
- 📄 **Instant document generation** — produce Appointment Letters, Experience Certificates, Offer Letters, Relieving Letters and 6 more in one click. Variables are auto-filled from employee records. Can also generate letters for **new hires or external candidates** not yet in the system. Every letter is emailed directly to the recipient in a professional format.
- 📊 **Leadership at a glance** — active headcount, present today, pending approvals, leave summary and department charts the moment you log in.
- 💰 **Payroll made simple** — set the salary structure once; turn PF/ESI/PT/Bonus/Gratuity on or off; payslips generate automatically.
- 📱 **Social media publishing** — post job openings and company updates directly to LinkedIn and Instagram from within HRMS, no third-party tool needed.
- 🎨 **A workspace people enjoy** — light/dark themes, background styles, accent colours, collapsible menu.
